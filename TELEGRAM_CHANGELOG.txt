Lawnchair 15 Beta 3 is here!

As many of you know, the Android launcher landscape is changing rapidly over the past few months. To welcome users looking for a new, open-source home, this release now <b>supports importing backups from Nova Launcher</b>!

What it imports
• Home screen and dock grid layout
• Widgets and folders
• Selected icon pack

How to use it
1. Open Lawnchair Settings
2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner
3. Select "Restore Nova backup" and choose your file

<b>Note:</b> Other Nova features not mentioned (like app drawer categories and icon swipe gestures) are not yet supported and won't be imported. You may need to do a little manual tweaking after the import.

While this is a major milestone for Lawnchair 15, our team is still working hard to improve and polish the Lawnchair 16 experience. Stay tuned for updates!
