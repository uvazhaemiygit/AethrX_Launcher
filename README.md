# AethrX Launcher
AetherX Launcher is a modern Android launcher that puts animation, smoothness, and beauty at the very core of its experience. Built on the trusted foundation of Lawnchair, it takes everything you love about stock Android and elevates it with silky-smooth transitions, elegant motion design, and a polished interface that feels alive in your hands.

Every swipe, every gesture, every animation is carefully crafted to deliver a fluid, responsive, and visually delightful experience — because your home screen should feel as good as it looks.

This project is proudly generated with the power of DeepSeek, combining human creativity with cutting-edge AI to bring you a launcher that's not just functional, but truly inspiring.

From the moment you unlock your device, AetherX wraps your phone in a layer of beauty and performance — because we believe that software should be both powerful and poetic.

Thank you for being here. Thank you for trusting us with your home screen.
